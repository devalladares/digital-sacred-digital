In a world fast moving into the digital realm,
into a overwhelming flurry of media and banner ads
constantly vying for time and attention, is there a
space online where one finds peace?

Sacred, Digital Sacred is a project by Dev Valladares
exploring the limits and possibilities of using design to
create of transcendence, in cyberspace. Spaces designed
to slow down; to created to evoke an appreciation
for life, the universe, and reality; and perhaps,
to ask the big questions.
